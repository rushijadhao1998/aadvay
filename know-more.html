About PURE India Trust

Aadvay Multipurpose Foundation started in April 2021, with a mission to provide quality Education & Employment opportunities in rural & semi-urban areas. We focus on below subjects:

Education

• Career Guidance & Scholarships • Students’ holistic development • School Infrastructure Development

Employment

• Entrepreneurship Development • Skill Development

Sustainability

• Water conservation • Plantation • Model Village

Vision

• Our vision is to become India’s highly trusted & preferred NGO partner to implement sustainable solution

Mission

• Create an ecosystem to support 10,000+ youth to become Entrepreneur by 2025. • Conduct Career Guidance Workshops for 100,000+ students & teachers by 2025.
